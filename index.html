How does it work?

We will assign you small design thinking exercises, we call them vibes. For every completed vibe you will be matched with a likeminded stranger for a 1:1 chat; We use the blockchain to guarantee and higher level of commitment between users. And don’t worry, we will break the ice for you!
Complete your profile to get your first match.

Who is it for?

Anyone who wants to build something and is looking for other makers to partner with.

  • 001 Hackathons

    Hackvibes focus is in creating a network of people and ideas to form a team and join hackathons. learn more

  • 002 Side projects

    It works well to discuss a side project with a fellow maker, and maybe start a collaboration! learn more

  • 003 Get feedback

    Stress test your ideas with strangers and get honest feedback learn more

    Hackvibes is a collective exercise in brainstorming, open to anyone! learn more
